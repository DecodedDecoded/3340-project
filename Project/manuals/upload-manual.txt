****** HOW TO UPLOAD CONTENT *****************

1. Click the Upload icon on the top bar.

2. CLick the button "Choose File" and then select the media you would like to upload from File Explorer.

3. Input your title in the field provided.

4.Input your description.

5. Select your privacy setting (public or private).

6. Select your category from the dropdown menu.

7. Click "Upload" to upload your content.
